Common Questions

FAQ

When should we arrive in Jamaica?

We recommend arriving at least one day before the wedding to relax and settle in. The resort offers plenty of activities to enjoy before the big day!

What's the weather like in July?

July in Jamaica is warm and tropical, with temperatures around 82-90°F (28-32°C). Pack light, breathable clothing and don't forget sunscreen!

Is the resort adults-only?

Yes, Sandals Royal Caribbean is an adults-only resort (18+), creating a romantic and peaceful atmosphere.

How do I book my stay?

Visit the Sandals website or contact their reservations team. Mention you're attending the "Jane & Joe Wedding" for our special group rates.
